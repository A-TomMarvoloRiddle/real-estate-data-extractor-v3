About this home

Roomy high floor Greenwich Village studio with a terrace at the historic Cast Iron Building. The apartment has a large living area along with a walk-in closet, full bath, second closet, built-in HVAC, and a private terrace. The kitchen is ready for your personal touch, with all connections available for your choice of appliances and cabinetry. There are an abundance of storage options and hardwood floors throughout. The long entrance hallway is perfect for a row of bookshelves for your personal library. The Cast Iron Building is a full-service pre-war co-op with a full time doorman and resident superintendent. Building amenities include a landscaped roof deck with iconic NYC views, common laundry room, bike room, gym and private storage. Sublets, gift purchasing, guarantors, co-purchasing and pied-a-terres are allowed on a case-by-case basis. Pets are allowed. 80% financing is permitted. In the heart of Greenwich Village, the building lies between Union Square and Washington Square Park with many transportation options. Nearby shopping options include Wegmans, Whole Foods, Trader Joes, and the iconic Strand Book Store. There are a multitude of dining, caf, cinema and nightlife options to suit your lifestyle.
